## üéØ **Success Strategies to Guarantee Passing**

### **For CompTIA A+:**
1. **Start with Professor Messer's videos** - Watch all 140 videos systematically
2. **Take practice tests regularly** - Use ExamCompass for consistent practice
3. **Focus on hands-on labs** - Set up virtual machines and practice troubleshooting
4. **Study both cores simultaneously** - Don't wait to finish Core 1 before starting Core 2
5. **Join study groups** - Professor Messer hosts regular study sessions

### **For ISC2 CC:**
1. **Complete the official ISC2 training** - The 14-hour adaptive course is essential
2. **Use the GitHub study guide** - BecomingCyber's repository is comprehensive
3. **Practice with flashcards** - ISC2's official flashcards are highly effective
4. **Take multiple practice tests** - Aim for consistent 80%+ scores
5. **Focus on the 5 domains equally** - Don't neglect any area

### **General Tips:**
- **Create a study schedule** - Dedicate 1-2 hours daily for 6-8 weeks
- **Use multiple resources** - Don't rely on just one source
- **Practice time management** - Both exams are timed
- **Join online communities** - Reddit, Discord, and official forums provide support
- **Schedule your exam** - Having a deadline creates accountability
